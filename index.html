1. Cats can jump up to 6 times their height.

2. They have a total of 18 toes.

4. Cats sleep for around 13 to 16 hours a day (70% of their life).
Have you ever noticed that cats are always sleeping?
They do this to save their energy. In the wild they would be saving their energy to go and hunt for their food. House cats obviously don’t need to do this but it just comes naturally to them.

5. 1 year of a cats life equals to 15 years of a humans live.
That’s right!
Cats age much faster than humans and live for a shorter amount of time. This means that when a cat is 1 year old, that actually equals to 15 human years!

6. One of the largest domestic cat breeds is a Maine Coon.
The Maine Coon is one of the largest domestic cat breeds there is.
In fact, the Guinness World Records gave Barivel, a Maine Coon, the award for being the longest domestic cat ever! Barivel is 120cm long and lives in Italy.

7. The smallest cat breed is a Singapura. It originates from Singapore. They are around half the size of a normal house cat.
